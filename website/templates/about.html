Welcome to Galactic Conquest

The leader of the Cosmic Empire, Galaxion, has seized control of 12 planets within the Milky Way Galaxy and intends to continue his conquest until he dominates the entire galaxy! Fortunately, you have been personally selected by the Galactic Federation to reclaim these captured worlds and protect the Milky Way from Galaxion's grip.
